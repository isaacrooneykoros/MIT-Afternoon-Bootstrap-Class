My Portfolio

At Brij Technologies Inc., I lead an innovative tech-driven journey to transform financial transactions across African economies. With a deep-rooted passion for progress, development, and integration, I aim to create value for our customers, investors, partners and to empower millions of people with access to secure, reliable, and affordable payment solutions. I leverage my extensive experience and skills in product development, business analysis, and agile project management to drive revenue growth and profitability for our organization. Collaborating with cross-functional teams and stakeholders, I have successfully launched and scaled multiple e-payment products and platforms, such as Jambopay Market, Government E-Revenue systems and QR Payments. I also chair the QR Payments Standardisation Committee at the Central Bank of Kenya, where I oversee the development and implementation of standards for using QR codes in payment systems within the country. I hold a Master of Science in Project Management from the University of Salford, where I gained valuable knowledge and insights on managing complex and dynamic projects.
